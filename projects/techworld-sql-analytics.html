2.3 · Monthly Revenue Trend with YoY Growth

A CTE was used alongside the LAG() window function to compare each month's revenue against the same month in the prior year. Data was partitioned by month number and ordered by year to compute accurate year-over-year growth percentages.

-- YoY growth using LAG() window function
WITH monthly AS (
  SELECT DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) AS month,
         SUM(total_amount) AS revenue
  FROM orders WHERE order_status NOT IN ('Cancelled', 'Returned')
  GROUP BY 1
)
SELECT month, revenue,
       LAG(revenue) OVER (PARTITION BY EXTRACT(month FROM month) ORDER BY month) AS prior_year,
       ROUND((revenue - LAG(revenue) OVER (...)) / LAG(revenue) OVER (...) * 100, 1) AS yoy_pct
FROM monthly ORDER BY 1;
⚠ Data Note: The 2025 records in this dataset cover January–May only. All 2025 YoY figures below are partial-year comparisons against a full 2024 — negative growth rates reflect incomplete data, not a business decline.

Part 3

SQL Techniques Applied

Each of the seven problems required a distinct analytical approach. The techniques below were selected based on what the data required — not used for their own sake.

1

Pre-Built Views

Five analytical views (vw_customer_lifetime_value, etc.) were used as the foundation for most queries — handling complex multi-table joins and filtering cancelled/returned orders consistently across all analyses.

2

LAG() Window Function

Used in the YoY trend analysis to compare each month's revenue to the same month in the prior year. Partitioned by month number and ordered by year, this avoided the need for self-joins and produced accurate period-over-period comparisons.

3

RANK() Window Function

Applied to the employee commission report to rank each sales employee within their regional territory — enabling simultaneous cross-region comparison while preserving regional context.

4

FILTER (WHERE ...) Aggregates

Used in the sales channel analysis to calculate multiple conditional aggregations (completed orders, cancellations, returns) across all channels in a single query pass — avoiding subqueries or CASE WHEN repetition.

5

CTEs with CROSS JOIN

Used in the segmentation analysis to compute revenue share percentages. One CTE summed revenue by segment; a second computed the grand total; a CROSS JOIN attached the grand total to all segment rows, enabling inline percentage calculations without a scalar subquery.

6

Direct 3-Table Joins

The return rate analysis required joining orders, order_items, and products directly rather than using a pre-built view, because no single view contained all the required fields in the required combination.

7

Volume-Weighted Margin Calculation

A custom metric — weighted margin — was calculated in the product analysis to reveal where lower-margin products were driving disproportionate sales volume (particularly visible in Gaming, where simple vs weighted margin diverged by 8 percentage points).

Business Recommendations

Actionable Insights for the Business

Five priority recommendation areas emerge directly from the analytical findings, each targeting a measurable business outcome.

👑

Customer Strategy

Implement dedicated account management for the top 10 Enterprise customers. Launch a Regular-to-Premium upgrade programme — Regular customers already spend $4,771 per head, more than Premium. Invest in Premium churn prevention: they contribute the most total revenue (34.9%).

📦

Product Strategy

Bundle accessories with laptops and smartphones — Accessories carry the highest margin (69.7%) but lowest revenue. Investigate Monitor quality and descriptions (16.7% return rate is unsustainable). Study Gaming's zero-return performance for lessons applicable to other categories.

📞

Sales Channel Strategy

Invest in the Phone channel — it generates the most revenue with the lowest problem rates. Urgently review In-Store operations (18.8% cancellation rate). Audit the Mobile App UX — a combined 29.6% cancellation and return rate suggests customers are being misled or making accidental orders.

👥

Workforce Strategy

Recognise and retain Luisa Fernandez — top commission earner with the highest AOV. Hire a second sales rep for Latin America (Maria Garcia is sole coverage — a continuity risk). Review commission structure: Sales Reps are out-earning their managers in every region.

📅

Seasonal Strategy

Maximise Q4 readiness — December is TechWorld's peak month. Inventory build-up and targeted campaigns should begin no later than September. Stimulate Q1 and Q3 with bundle deals and channel-specific promotions to smooth revenue seasonality.
